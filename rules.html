Community Rules

  • 1. Be respectful and kind
    • Respect others in the community. Disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
  • 2. No harassment
    • Harassment or threats will lead to immediate action. This is a safe space.
  • 3. No unauthorized bots
    • Only use bots that are approved. Unauthorized bots may compromise the platform.
  • 4. No spammers
    • Avoid spamming messages or links. Repetitive content clutters the chat.
  • 5. Be chill
    • Keep the conversation relaxed and friendly. Aggression is not welcomed here.
  • 6. Use the channels correctly
    • Follow the guidelines of each channel. Misuse of channels disrupts the flow of communication.
  • 7. No promoting
    • Do not promote other servers, products, or services without permission.
